[English]--

Welcome to Hong Kong!

The map is based on of "Yau Ma Tei" in Hong Kong.

Because of the biochemical attack in the area, the citizens have turned into zombies.

The Hong Kong government has decided to enclose the entire epidemic area, and the citizens inside the wall can only find a way to survive.

How many days can you survive?

[中文]--

歡迎來到香港！

地圖設定在香港「油麻地」的地區，因為區內受到生化襲擊，市民都變成喪屍，港府決定將整個疫區圍封，牆內市民只能自己想辦法繼續生存。

在香港這類高密集的環境當中，你可以預見到處都會有大量殭屍。
